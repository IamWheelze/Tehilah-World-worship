About Tehilah World Worship

A global gathering of prayer and worship across nations.

What It Is

Tehilah World Worship is a global prayer and worship movement uniting believers from every nation. Over 21 days in April 1325, we come together daily in prayer, culminating in 12 hours of worship on April 13–14.

Tehilah (Hebrew: תהילה) means "praise" — specifically, the kind of praise that God inhabits. This event is a declaration that God is worthy of praise from every tongue, tribe, and nation.

Why We Are Doing This

We believe that when the Body of Christ unites across borders, languages, and cultures to lift one sound of worship, heaven responds. This is not just an event — it is a prophetic act of faith.

  • To create a continuous wave of prayer and worship across time zones
  • To unite believers globally in a shared spiritual experience
  • To declare God's sovereignty over every nation
  • To intercede prophetically for the nations of the earth
  • To raise a new generation of worshippers who carry the fire of God

How It Works

Phase 1: 19 Days of Daily Prayer (the lead-up days)

Each day we gather online for 1 hour of focused, faith-filled prayer. Different nations lead different sessions, creating a beautiful tapestry of global intercession.

Phase 2: 12 Hours of Worship (April 13–14)

Beginning Friday, April 13 at 6:00 PM CET and ending Tuesday, April 14 at 6:00 AM CET, we worship non-stop for 12 hours. Different teams from around the world lead worship in shifts, ensuring the praise never stops.

The final 4 hours are dedicated to prophetic prayers for nations — a powerful time of intercession and declaration.
