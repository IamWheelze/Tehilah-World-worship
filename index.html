How It Works

A two-phase movement uniting nations in prayer and worship.

Phase 1 — Complete

The 24-Hour Charge

March 20 – 21, 2026

19 Days of Daily Prayer as the lead-up — each day we gathered online for 1 hour of focused, faith-filled prayer. Different nations led different sessions, creating a beautiful tapestry of global intercession. It culminated in a 24-Hour Charge on March 20–21.

Phase 2 — Coming

The Glory — 12-Hour Charge

April 13 – 14, 2026

A concentrated 12-hour encounter with the glory of God — worship, intercession, and prophetic declaration across nations. Register now and join believers from every corner of the earth.
