Video Submissions

Send us a short video of your worship, prayer, or testimony.

Video Guidelines

  • Duration: 30–60 seconds
  • Orientation: Landscape (horizontal)
  • Audio: Clear and audible
  • Language: English, French, or Russian
  • Content: Worship, prayer, testimony, or greeting from your nation
  • Include your name and country at the beginning

How to Submit

  • Post on Instagram or TikTok and tag @tehilahworldworship
  • Or send the file via the Contact page with the subject "Video Submission"
  • Videos may be featured in the Media Hub and on our social channels
